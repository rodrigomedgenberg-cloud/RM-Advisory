The Approach

A revenue operating system built on judgment — accelerated by AI.

Where AI does the heavy lifting

Discovery prep & research — AI synthesises company financials, org charts, regulatory filings, and competitive landscapes before your rep even picks up the phone.
Competitive intelligence — Real-time monitoring of competitor pricing, product launches, and market positioning across fintech and SaaS landscapes. Automated, not manual.
CRM hygiene & pipeline analysis — AI flags stuck deals, missing next steps, misaligned stages, and sandbagging across regions. Weekly, automatically.
Content & outbound at speed — Personalised sequences, proposal drafts, and meeting prep for institutional clients or enterprise buyers — generated in minutes, not hours.
Sales controlling & dashboards — Automated performance visibility across regions, products, and client segments so leadership makes decisions from data, not gut feel.

Where humans remain irreplaceable

Deal strategy & qualification judgment — Is this deal real? Is the champion actually a champion? Is the compelling event manufactured or genuine? This takes pattern recognition from thousands of deals.
Reading the room — Organisational politics, executive body language, the unsaid objection, the blocker nobody named. No model sees this.
Coaching & developing people — Helping a rep evolve from tactical seller to strategic operator. Building confidence after a lost deal. Knowing when to push and when to back off.
Designing the system itself — Which stages, which fields, which cadence, which metrics matter for this specific business at this specific stage. Context is everything.
Accountability under pressure — Operating under board and investor scrutiny. Making the call when the data is ambiguous. Owning the number.
Operating Philosophy

Take what works from every framework. Discard the dogma.

I

Stages and exit criteria, not methodology worship

MEDDPICC, Sandler, Challenger — each captures something real. We extract the fields that matter (compelling event, champion, economic buyer, close plan) and embed them into clearly defined stages with customer-verified exit criteria. Then we use AI to auto-populate and enforce them. Structure without religion.

II

AI-augmented systems, human-owned decisions

We teach your team to use AI for research, prep, and pipeline hygiene — the work that used to eat 40% of a rep's week. Then we redeploy that time into the activities only humans can do: relationship building, deal strategy, and executive conversations.

III

Skill capacity is the real bottleneck

Knowing what to do isn't enough. Your team needs the ability to execute — discovery, multi-threading, champion development, executive alignment. We build this through coaching, deal reviews, and deliberate practice. AI accelerates learning; humans do the learning.
